Who We Are

Chaffee Carpentry is an owner-operated, full service building and remodeling construction company based in Billerica, Massachusetts and servicing surrounding towns including Burlington, Bedford, Winchester, and Lexington.

ABOUT CHAFFEE CARPENTRY
We have been in business since 1988, servicing more than 200 clients. We are fully licensed and insured in Massachusetts, including Worker's Compensation and Liability Insurance.

BRAD CHAFFEE
Owner-operator with 25+ years of experience in the construction industry. Licensed in Massachusetts. Home Improvement Contractor Registration #100788, Construction Supervisor License #52807. EPA lead-safe trained and certified. Attended Northeastern University and Wentworth Institute.
